About Me

The first time I encountered computer science was as a freshman in high school. I took classes throughout high school in programming, data structures, web development, and app development. I was fortunate to be given the opportunity to explore the field early on and it did not take me long to realize that it was the field of study for me.

In my free time I like to play music. I have played the violin since I was in first grade and although I no longer play in an orchestra, the violin and the classical music that often accompanies it are still some of my greatest joys. Since coming to college guitar and piano have been more accessible than the violin and provide better means for expanding my understanding of music theory. I will always love playing and sharing music with other people.

While not studying or playing music I run middle distance for the Tufts Track & Field team. I started running in high school and the daily ritual of training for competition is something I cannot imagine living without.
